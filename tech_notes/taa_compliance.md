# TAA Compliance

The US Federal Government is interested in limiting procurement of products and services to Countries with active Trade Agreements.

* [Trade Agreements Act (TAA)][2] Wikipedia overview.

## Trade Agreements Designated Countries

In the Federal Aquisition Regulation (FAR) a list of Designated Countries is maintained. This list is subject to change - always check the source for changes.

* FAR Section [25.003 Definitions][3]
  * Find section with heading "*Designated country* means any of the following countries"
* FAR Section [52.225-5 Trade Agreements][1]
  * Find the section with heading "*Designated country* means any of the following countries"

[1]: https://www.acquisition.gov/far/part-52#FAR_52_225_5
[2]: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trade_Agreements_Act_of_1979
[3]: https://www.acquisition.gov/far/25.003
